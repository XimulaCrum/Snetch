<b>What it does</b>
Snetch is a queue-first downloader for Android built on top of yt-dlp. It is designed for people who keep adding links and want the app to stay readable instead of turning into a pile of parallel work.

<b>Main features</b>
- Download video and audio from sites supported by yt-dlp
- Queue-first workflow with sequential downloads by default
- Saved presets for repeat downloads
- Playlist links can expand into individual queued items
- Wi-Fi-aware queueing for metered-network control
- Format selection, subtitles, thumbnails, metadata, and custom command templates

<b>Why this fork exists</b>
Many Android downloaders look fast until they start too much work at once. Snetch leans in the opposite direction: calmer queue handling, clearer task states, and fewer surprises on real devices.

<b>Notes for F-Droid users</b>
- F-Droid builds do not use the in-app auto-update flow
- The app includes no Google Play Services dependency
- Some websites may still require cookies or a logged-in session before downloads will work
